Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MailBouncer does not collect, store, or transmit your emails to external servers. All data stays within your Google account.

Information We Access

To provide its functionality, MailBouncer requires access to:

1. Gmail (Read & Modify)

We read email metadata (sender, subject, date) to categorize incoming emails. We apply Gmail labels and archive messages to organize your inbox.

Scope: gmail.modify, gmail.labels — We never read email body content for categorization. Only sender addresses and subjects are analyzed.

2. Current Message

When you open an email, we read the sender info to show their status (approved, blocked, unknown) in the sidebar card.

Scope: gmail.addons.current.message.readonly — Limited to the message you have open.

3. Script Triggers

We install a time-driven trigger that runs once per hour to automatically screen new emails, even when Gmail is closed.

Scope: script.scriptapp — Used only to manage the screening trigger.

Information We Store

Sender Lists

Your approved and blocked sender lists are stored via Google's PropertiesService (UserProperties). This data is within Google's infrastructure, associated with your account, and not accessible to us or anyone else.

Settings & State

Your preferences and the timestamp of the last screening run are stored in UserProperties. No external database is involved.

What We Don't Collect

Email content or body text
Attachments
Personal information
Analytics or tracking
Third-party data sharing
Advertising data
Contact lists
Browsing history

No External Servers

MailBouncer runs entirely within Google's infrastructure (Apps Script). There are no external servers, no databases, no API calls to third-party services. Your data never leaves Google.

Data Security & Deletion

All data is protected by Google's security infrastructure. To delete all MailBouncer data, simply uninstall the add-on. Your Gmail labels will remain (you can delete them manually) but all stored preferences and sender lists will be removed.

Google API Compliance

MailBouncer's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.
