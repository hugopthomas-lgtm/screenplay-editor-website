2. Description of Service

MailBouncer is a Google Workspace Add-on for Gmail that automatically screens unknown senders, categorizes newsletters and receipts, and helps you manage your inbox. The Add-on runs within Google's infrastructure using Google Apps Script.

3. Account Requirements

You must have a Google account with Gmail to use MailBouncer. By using the Add-on, you authorize it to access your Gmail account as described in our Privacy Policy.

4. How It Works

MailBouncer creates Gmail labels to organize your email. It uses a time-driven trigger (once per hour) to automatically process new incoming emails. Emails from unknown senders are moved out of your inbox and labeled for your review. You can approve or block senders at any time.

The Add-on categorizes emails based on sender addresses, subject lines, and email metadata. No email body content is read for categorization purposes.
