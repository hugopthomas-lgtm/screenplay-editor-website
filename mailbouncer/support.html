Need help? We've got you covered.

Getting Started

After installing MailBouncer from the Marketplace, open Gmail and click the MailBouncer icon in the right sidebar. Click "Enable MailBouncer" to start. The bouncer will auto-approve your existing contacts and start screening new senders within the hour.

FAQ

What is the Screener?

The Screener intercepts emails from unknown senders. Instead of landing in your inbox, they wait in a holding area (MailBouncer/Screener label). You decide: approve them to your Inbox, Feed, or Paper Trail, or block them permanently.

Does it work when Gmail is closed?

Yes. MailBouncer uses a server-side trigger that runs every hour, even when your browser is closed or your computer is off. Emails arriving at 3 AM will be screened before you wake up.

What is the Feed?

Newsletters, digests, and marketing emails are automatically detected and routed to the Feed (MailBouncer/Feed label). They're there when you want to read them, but they don't clutter your inbox.

What is Paper Trail?

Receipts, order confirmations, shipping updates, and billing emails are auto-sorted into Paper Trail (MailBouncer/PaperTrail label). Easy to find when you need them, invisible when you don't.

How does Reply Later work?

When reading an email, click "Reply Later" in the MailBouncer sidebar card. The email gets labeled (MailBouncer/ReplyLater) and appears in your Reply Later pile on the dashboard.

Will I miss important emails?

On first install, MailBouncer auto-approves all senders already in your inbox. Only truly new, never-seen-before senders get screened. Check your Screener queue regularly to approve legitimate new contacts.

How do I approve or block a sender?

Two ways: (1) From the Dashboard card, review pending senders and click "Inbox", "Feed", or "Block". (2) When reading an email from an unknown sender, use the buttons in the contextual card.

Can I undo a block?

Currently, you'd need to find the sender's email in the MailBouncer/Blocked label and manually move it back. A full sender management interface is coming soon.

Is MailBouncer free?

Yes. MailBouncer is completely free. No account required beyond your Google account. No ads, no tracking, no premium tier.

How do I uninstall?

Go to Google Workspace Marketplace, find MailBouncer, and click "Uninstall". The Gmail labels will remain (delete them manually if you want) but all settings and sender lists will be removed.

Is my data safe?

MailBouncer runs entirely within Google's infrastructure. No external servers, no databases, no third-party services. Your email data never leaves Google. See our Privacy Policy.
