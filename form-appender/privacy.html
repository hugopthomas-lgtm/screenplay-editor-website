Form Appender — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

What Form Appender does

Form Appender is a Google Forms add-on that automatically appends form submission responses to Google Docs in a Drive folder of your choice.

Data we access

Form Appender accesses the following data solely to perform its function:

  • Google Forms data — question titles and submitted responses, to format and append them to Docs.
  • Google Drive (read-only) — to list Google Docs in your selected folder and validate folder access.
  • Google Docs — to append formatted responses to the destination document(s).
  • Respondent email — optionally included in the appended output, only if you enable the "Include respondent email" option.

Data storage

Your configuration (selected folder, questions, format preferences) is stored in Google Apps Script's DocumentProperties, which is scoped to your specific Form. This data is stored by Google on its servers and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Form Appender does not store, transmit, or collect any data on external servers. There is no backend, no database, and no analytics.

Data sharing

Form Appender does not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties. All processing happens within your Google account using Google Apps Script.

Permissions explained

  • forms.currentonly — Read questions and listen for submissions on the current form only.
  • documents — Write formatted responses to Google Docs.
  • drive.readonly — List Docs in your selected Drive folder.
  • script.container.ui — Display the configuration sidebar.
  • script.scriptapp — Install and manage the form submission trigger.

Data retention

Configuration data persists as long as the Form exists. You can delete all Form Appender data at any time using the "Reset configuration" button in the sidebar. Uninstalling the add-on also removes all stored configuration.

Children's privacy

Form Appender is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
