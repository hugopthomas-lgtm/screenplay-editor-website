Support

Need help with Form Appender? Start here.

Getting Started

After installing from the Google Workspace Marketplace, open any Google Form, go to Add-ons → Form Appender → Configure. Pick a folder, map a question, choose your format, and activate the trigger. Every new response will be automatically appended to the right Google Doc.

FAQ

How do I set up Form Appender?

Open your Google Form, go to Add-ons, select Form Appender, and click Configure. Pick a Drive folder containing your target Docs, map one form question to select the document, choose which fields to include, and activate the trigger.

What are the output formats?

Three options: Bullets (clean bulleted list per submission), Vertical table (one table per submission with question-answer rows), or Horizontal table (one growing table with a row per submission).

How does the doc-picker question work?

One of your form questions must be a multiple choice or dropdown. Form Appender populates it with the names of Google Docs from your selected folder. The respondent's choice determines which Doc the response is appended to.

Can I choose which questions are appended?

Yes. During configuration, you can select or deselect individual form questions. Only the ones you check will appear in the output.

Is Form Appender free?

Yes, Form Appender is completely free. No premium tier, no limits, no ads.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All processing happens within your Google account. No data is sent to external servers, no data is collected, no tracking.

Can I use it with multiple forms?

Yes. Install and configure Form Appender independently on each Google Form. Each form can target a different folder and have its own settings.

What happens if I delete a target Doc?

If a target Doc no longer exists, the response for that submission won't be appended. Other submissions going to other Docs are not affected.

Does it work with existing responses?

Form Appender processes new submissions after the trigger is activated. It does not retroactively process responses submitted before setup.

How do I stop or remove Form Appender?

Go to Add-ons → Form Appender → Configure, and deactivate the trigger. To fully remove it, uninstall the add-on from your Google Form's Add-ons menu.
