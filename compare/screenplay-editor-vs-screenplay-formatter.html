Screenplay Editor vs Screenplay Formatter

Last updated: February 2026

This isn't really a comparison. Screenplay Formatter is gone. It was the most popular screenplay add-on for Google Docs, with over 1 million installs. Then it disappeared. Here's what happened.

What Happened to Screenplay Formatter?

Screenplay Formatter was the go-to screenplay formatting tool for Google Docs. Simple, free, reliable. Over a million writers installed it. Film schools recommended it. It was the default.

Then Google changed its APIs and extension policies. Screenplay Formatter stopped working. It was removed from the Google Workspace Marketplace. No announcement from the developer. No timeline for a return. Google Support threads filled up with confused users asking where it went.

As of February 2026, Screenplay Formatter is still gone. There is no sign it will come back.

What Screenplay Editor Offers

Screenplay Editor was built to fill exactly this gap. Same philosophy: a simple add-on that formats screenplays inside Google Docs. Same click-to-format approach. No markup language. No new app to learn. But with more features than Screenplay Formatter ever had.
