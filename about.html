About

Screenplay Editor is a Google Docs add-on built for screenwriters who want professional formatting without the complexity of traditional screenwriting software.

Why Screenplay Editor?

Most screenwriting tools force you into their ecosystem: proprietary file formats, expensive subscriptions, desktop-only software. Screenplay Editor takes a different approach: it brings industry-standard screenplay formatting directly to Google Docs.

Write anywhere. Collaborate in real-time. Export to PDF or .fdx when you're ready. Your work stays in your Google Drive, always accessible, always yours.

Who made this?

I'm Hugo, a screenwriter and filmmaker based in France. I built Screenplay Editor because I was tired of paying for bloated software just to write. I wanted something simple: open Google Docs, write, format, done.

No company behind this. Just me, a laptop, and way too many hours obsessing over margins and indentation so you don't have to.

If you have feedback, ideas, or just want to say hi, I read every email.

Open & Transparent

Screenplay Editor is free to use. It doesn't collect your data, doesn't require an account beyond your Google account, and doesn't lock your scripts into a proprietary format.
