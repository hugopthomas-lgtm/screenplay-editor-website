Hugo Thomas
Writer & Director

I'm Hugo Thomas, a French writer and director. My first feature film, Willy 1er, premiered at Cannes. Juniors, my second baby, screened at festivals worldwide. They're both on Netflix. Before that, my shorts Perrault, La Fontaine, Mon Cul! and Ich Bin Eine Tata both won awards at Clermont-Ferrand, the world's largest short film festival in the world (did I mention it's the largest?).

I've spent years formatting scripts, fighting with software that wasn't built for how writers actually work. Final Draft feels like the 90s to me. I do love vintage aesthetics, but sending license codes by email every time you need to collaborate? That's where I draw the line.

This is the tool I wish I had when I started. Simple, fast, and built by someone who actually writes. I built Screenplay Editor because professional tools shouldn't cost a fortune or get in the way of writing.
