Who made this?

I'm Hugo Thomas, a French writer and director based in Rome. My first feature film, Willy 1er, premiered at the Cannes Film Festival in the ACID selection (2016). Juniors, my second, screened at festivals worldwide. Both are on Netflix. Before that, my shorts Perrault, La Fontaine, Mon Cul! and Ich Bin Eine Tata both won awards at Clermont-Ferrand, the world's largest short film festival in the world (did I mention it's the largest?).

I've spent years formatting scripts, fighting with software that wasn't built for how writers actually work. Final Draft feels like the 90s to me. I do love vintage aesthetics, but sending license codes by email every time you need to collaborate? That's where I draw the line.

The team behind Screenplay Editor

I brought together fellow screenwriters to test every feature and give honest feedback, and developers who helped me build this over several months. The result is simple, fast, and made with a lot of care.
