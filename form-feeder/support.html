Support

Need help with Form Feeder? Start here.

Getting Started

After you install from the Google Workspace Marketplace, open any Google Form and go to Add-ons → Form Feeder → Configurer. Pick a question, choose your Google Sheet through the Picker, point at the column that holds the values, and save. The question's choices now come straight from your Sheet.

FAQ

How do I set up Form Feeder?

Open your form, go to Add-ons, select Form Feeder, and click Configurer. Click a question in the list, choose a Google Sheet with the Picker, pick the tab and column, set sorting and de-duplication if you want, and save. Form Feeder fills the choices right away.

Which question types work?

Dropdowns, multiple choice, and checkboxes get their options from your column. Grids get their rows. Other question types (short answer, date, scale, and so on) cannot hold a list, so they are not linkable.

Do the choices update on their own?

Yes, if you turn it on. You can refresh by hand from the sidebar, after every form submission, or on a timer (every 5, 15, 30, or 60 minutes). When the column in your Sheet changes, the form follows.

Can I sort the choices or remove duplicates?

Yes. Each link has a sort option (A to Z, Z to A, or the Sheet's own order) and a de-duplication toggle. You can preview the exact list before you save.

Can one form pull from several Sheets?

Yes. Each question links to its own Sheet, tab, and column. Different questions on the same form can read from different Sheets.

Does Form Feeder change my Sheet?

No. It reads your Sheet only. It never writes to it. The only thing it edits is the choices on your form question.

Is Form Feeder free?

Yes, completely free. No premium tier, no limits, no ads.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs inside your Google account. Form Feeder reads only the one Sheet you pick, sends nothing to any outside server, and does not read the answers people submit.

What if the column is empty or the Sheet is gone?

If the column has no values, Form Feeder leaves the question as it is and notes the issue in the sidebar. If you moved or deleted the Sheet, open the link and pick the Sheet again with the Picker.

How do I stop or remove Form Feeder?

Turn off the automatic refresh in the sidebar, and remove any links you no longer want. To remove it entirely, uninstall the add-on from your form's Add-ons menu.
