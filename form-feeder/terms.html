1. Description of Service

Form Feeder is a free Google Forms add-on. It reads values from a column in a Google Sheet you select, and sets them as the choices of a form question (dropdown, multiple choice, checkboxes, or grid). It can refresh those choices by hand, after each submission, or on a timer. The add-on operates within Google Forms, Google Sheets, and Google Drive.
