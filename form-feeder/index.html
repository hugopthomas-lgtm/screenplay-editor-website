Form Feeder

Form Feeder fills a Google Forms question from a column in your Google Sheet, and keeps the choices up to date on their own.

How it works

1

Pick the question to fill

Open the sidebar and choose a question. It works with dropdowns, multiple choice, checkboxes, and grids.

2

Choose a Sheet and a column

Select your Google Sheet through the Google Picker, then point at the tab and column that holds the values.

3

Sort and dedupe if you want

Order the choices A to Z, drop duplicates, and preview the exact list before you save.

4

Let it refresh on its own

Refresh by hand, after every submission, or on a timer. When the Sheet changes, the form follows.

When it helps

#

Lists that change

You keep workshops, slots, or products in a Sheet, and the form should always show the current ones.

~

Many people edit

Your team updates the Sheet. Nobody has to reopen the form editor to keep the options right.

=

Long option lists

You have dozens of choices. You maintain them in one column instead of retyping them in Forms.

Free and private

Form Feeder is free. It reads only the one Sheet you pick, runs inside your Google account, and sends nothing to any outside server.
